LC25

10-band inductor graphic EQ · 25 Hz – 12.8 kHz

The LC25 sits at the bottom end of the LC family, covering the bass and lower-mid frequencies that define the body of a mix. Hand-wound inductors give every band a smooth, slightly resonant character — the same flavour found in the broadcast EQs of the 1970s.

  • 10 fixed-frequency bands, 25Hz to 12.8kHz
  • ±15 dB cut/boost per band
